Privacy Policy

Chromatrix

Last updated: March 2026

Chromatrix respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share personal information.

Data Collection

Chromatrix does not collect personal data, analytics data, location data, contacts, browsing history, usage data, or device identifiers.

Local Data

The app may store limited information locally on your device, such as:

  • best score
  • basic game progress
  • local game settings

This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Chromatrix does not use third-party advertising networks, analytics tools, or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or from any other users.

Offline Use

Chromatrix works without creating an account and does not require users to sign in.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be posted on this page.
