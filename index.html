About the Game

Chromatrix is a fast-paced puzzle game where players find four matching corners that form squares or rectangles on a colorful grid. Larger shapes reward more points while the timer adds pressure.

Privacy Policy

Chromatrix does not collect, store, or share personal data. The game runs entirely on your device.
